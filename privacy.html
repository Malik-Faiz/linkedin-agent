This Privacy Policy describes how AI Social Agent ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our automated social media posting service. By using the app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

The short version: We store only what we need to run your automated posts. We never sell your data. Your API keys and OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest. You can delete your data at any time.
01

📋 Information We Collect

We collect the minimum information required to provide the service:

  • Account credentials — your username and a salted SHA-256 hash of your password. We never store your password in plain text.
  • API keys — Groq API key and SerpAPI key you provide in Settings, encrypted at rest using Fernet symmetric encryption.
  • OAuth tokens — access tokens issued by LinkedIn, Facebook, and Instagram when you connect those accounts. These are encrypted at rest and never shared.
  • Post subjects — the topics and subjects you add to your posting queue.
  • Timezone offset — used to schedule posts at your local time.
  • Activity logs — timestamped records of posting actions (successes and errors) stored locally and shown in your dashboard.
02

⚙️ How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to operate the service on your behalf:

  • Authenticating your login and securing your session
  • Generating LinkedIn posts via the Groq AI API using your subjects
  • Fetching relevant images via SerpAPI when you request them
  • Publishing posts to the social media platforms you have connected
  • Running the scheduler to post at your configured times
  • Displaying your posting history and logs in the dashboard

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the features you use.

03

🔗 Third-Party Platforms

When you connect social accounts, you authorise this app to act on your behalf using tokens issued by those platforms. We interact with the following third-party APIs:

🔵 LinkedIn API 📘 Facebook Graph API 📷 Instagram Graph API
  • OAuth tokens are scoped to the minimum permissions needed (post creation only).
  • We never access your contacts, messages, or personal feed content.
  • Each platform's own privacy policy governs how they handle your data.
  • You can revoke our access at any time from within each platform's security settings.

Post content is also generated using the Groq API (LLaMA model) — your subject text is sent to Groq's servers to generate post copy. Please review Groq's Privacy Policy for details.

04

🔐 Data Storage & Security

  • All user data is stored on Railway's infrastructure in encrypted volumes.
  • Sensitive fields (API keys, OAuth tokens) are encrypted with Fernet AES-128 encryption before being written to disk.
  • Passwords are never stored — only a salted SHA-256 hash is kept.
  • Sessions are signed with a server-side secret key and expire on logout.
  • All communication with our server is over HTTPS (TLS).
  • User data directories are isolated per account — no user can access another user's data.
Despite these measures, no system is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password and to disconnect social accounts when not in use.
05

🗓️ Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account exists. Specifically:

  • Account data — retained until you delete your account.
  • Post subjects — consumed and removed from the queue as posts are published.
  • Activity logs — capped at the last 100 entries per user, automatically rolling.
  • OAuth tokens — stored until you disconnect the channel or delete your account.

To request deletion of your account and all associated data, contact us using the details below. We will process deletion within 30 days.

06

✋ Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction — update incorrect information via the Settings page.
  • Deletion — request full deletion of your account and data.
  • Portability — request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Restriction — ask us to stop processing your data while a dispute is resolved.
  • Objection — object to processing where we rely on legitimate interests.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via the email below. We will respond within 30 days.

07

🍪 Cookies & Sessions

We use a single server-side session cookie to keep you logged in. This cookie:

  • Is signed and encrypted — it cannot be forged or read by third parties.
  • Contains only your username and session state — no tracking data.
  • Is cleared immediately when you log out.
  • Does not track you across other websites.

We do not use advertising cookies, analytics trackers, or any third-party tracking scripts.

08

👶 Children's Privacy

This service is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EU/EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
